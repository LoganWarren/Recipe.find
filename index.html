Get Started

How To Use

  1. Enter the ingredients you have available in the input field (e.g., "chicken, tomatoes, onions").
  2. Click the "Find Recipes" button.
  3. Browse the suggested recipes and click on a recipe card to view detailed instructions.
  4. Follow the step-by-step instructions to prepare your meal.

Warning:

If no recipes appear, the API has reached its daily quota and will take 24 hours to reset.

About Recipes.find()

Recipes.find() is a web app designed to help you discover new and exciting recipes using the ingredients you already have in your pantry. Our goal is to make cooking more enjoyable and efficient by providing personalized recipe suggestions that cater to your dietary preferences and available ingredients. Join us on this culinary adventure and let's create some delicious meals together!
